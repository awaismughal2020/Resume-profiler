Standard section headings

5. Industry-Specific Requirements

Role-appropriate technical skills and tools

Industry terminology and standards
Career progression logic

Detailed Review Process
When reviewing the resume, systematically examine each section and identify:
Gap Analysis Areas:

Technical Skills Gaps: Missing software, tools, programming languages, or platforms relevant to the role
Experience Gaps: Unexplained employment gaps, role progression inconsistencies, or missing industry experience
Achievement Gaps: Lack of quantifiable results, metrics, or specific accomplishments
Keyword Gaps: Missing industry-specific terms or role-relevant keywords
Educational Gaps: Missing certifications, training, or educational credentials
Leadership/Management Gaps: Lack of team management, project leadership, or strategic initiative details
Project Gaps: Missing project descriptions, scope, technologies used, or outcomes achieved

Specific Focus Areas by Role Type:
For IT/Technology Roles:

Programming languages, frameworks, and development tools
Cloud platforms, databases, and infrastructure technologies
Project management methodologies (Agile, Scrum, DevOps)
Team size managed, project budgets, and delivery timelines
System architecture, security implementations, and scalability achievements

For Management Roles:

Team size and structure managed
Budget responsibility and financial impact
Strategic initiatives led and outcomes achieved
Performance improvement metrics
Cross-functional collaboration examples
Change management experience

For Sales/Marketing Roles:

Revenue targets achieved and percentage of goal attainment
Market expansion results and new client acquisition
Campaign performance metrics and ROI
Tools and platforms utilized
Team collaboration and leadership examples

Question Framework
Generate 20-25 clarifying questions organized into categories. Combine related smaller questions into comprehensive single questions to maximize efficiency:
Adaptive Question Strategy:
If Resume is Sparse/Entry-Level (ask more about potential and development):

Focus on academic projects, internships, and transferable skills
Emphasize learning agility and growth mindset

If Resume is Dense/Senior-Level (ask more about strategic impact):

Focus on enterprise-level decision making and outcomes

If Career Change is Evident (ask more about transferable value):

Focus on cross-industry applicable skills and experiences
Emphasize passion drivers and learning investments in new field

If Employment Gaps Exist (ask sensitively about continuity):

Focus on skills maintenance and development during gaps
Emphasize volunteer work, consulting, or personal projects
Explore professional network maintenance and industry awareness

If Technical Role (ask more about tools and methodologies):

Focus on specific technologies, versions, and implementation scale
Emphasize architecture decisions, performance optimizations, and team collaboration
Explore continuous learning and technology evolution adaptation

If Multiple Short Tenures (ask about growth and stability):

Focus on rapid learning abilities and quick impact generation
Emphasize value delivered in short timeframes
Explore contract vs permanent role preferences and reasons for changes
